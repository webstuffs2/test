Understanding Caffeine Consumption

The FDA recommends a maximum of 400mg of caffeine per day for healthy adults. Excessive caffeine can lead to negative effects such as:

  • Increased heart rate and blood pressure
  • Insomnia and restlessness
  • Digestive issues
  • Dependency and withdrawal symptoms

How to Reduce Caffeine Intake

If you're consuming too much caffeine, consider these strategies:

  • Gradually switch to decaf beverages
  • Replace energy drinks with natural alternatives like herbal tea
  • Drink more water to stay hydrated
  • Monitor hidden sources of caffeine, including chocolate and some medications
