Caffeine Overdose Calculator

Are you drinking too much caffeine? This calculator estimates how many caffeinated drinks would be dangerous for your body weight.

Understanding Caffeine Limits

The FDA suggests a daily limit of 400mg of caffeine for healthy adults. Consuming more can cause side effects like insomnia, nervousness, and rapid heart rate. A lethal dose is estimated at around 150mg per kg of body weight.

How to Reduce Caffeine Intake

  • Gradually switch to decaf alternatives.
  • Drink more water to stay hydrated.
  • Monitor caffeine sources, including chocolate and medication.
